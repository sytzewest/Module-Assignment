About the course

What is Aerospace Engineering ?

A programme in Aerospace Engineering prepares the student for the challenging environment of the aeronautics industry. The processes of critical thinking, dynamic teamwork and problem-solving will be taught alonside the practical knowledge required to excel in this field of study.

Students are also trained at large companies in the aerospace industry during an extended course internship.
