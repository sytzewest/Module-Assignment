Campus Life

Leadership of Osprey

Osprey is led by a board of govoners that are extremely well versed in the leading of an university. The leaders and the staff are the most competent people hand selected for leading the teachers, the teachers are highly educated personel with a passion for teaching , therefore all students are in the most capable hands in Emmen.

Beautiful surroundings and fantastic facilities

Everything on campus is interconnected and linked to the overall vitalistic beliefs of the people who live, study, teach and work here. Located in Emmen, a north of The Netherlands, the Osprey University campus offers the benefits of a big city along with the benefits of a serene outdoor environment. Whether you’re looking for the energy of an engaged campus community and an exelent board of directors, Osprey has the perfect mix of both.

Diversity

Our university has students from 20 diffrent nationalities. And because our university is international, 90% of total students are foreigners. Regardless of your nationality, you will always feel welcomed and like at home.

Student clubs and student counsel

We have a lot of student clubs like: football, basketball, robotics, debate club and so much more. Also our student counsel is always ready to help students.

Canteen

Our canteen has a big variety of diffrent kinds of meals, to satisfy the tastes of our students. It doesn't matter if you're vegetarian, vegan or pescatarian there is always something you can choose to eat.
