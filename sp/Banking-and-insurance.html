About the course

What is banking and insurance ?

A programme in Banking and Insurance is developed as per the requirements of the finance Industry where students learn banking operations, regulations, monetary auditing, selling of financial products and services. The programme curriculum includes studies of economics, management as well as laws of financial sector.

Students are also trained at Insurance companies and Banking Institutions during course internship. The management programme is offered at bachelor degree level. This specialised programme offers learning of mid-level expertise of the Banking and Insurance Industry.
