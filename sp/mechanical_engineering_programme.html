First year

The curriculum covers a wide range of subjects related to mechanical engineering:

  • product analysis
  • mechanical design and engineering
  • energy technology
  • materials and manufacturing technology
  • mathematics
  • energy related topics

Second year

During the first year you will lay a broad foundation in disciplines such as:

  • mathematics & modelling
  • mechanics & materials
  • design & manufacturing and thermodynamics
  • life-cycle assessment

In addition to that, about 40 percent of your time will be dedicated to application-oriented team projects and workshops.

Third year

In the first semester of the 3rd year you will do your first internship: you then work and study in a company related to your study field in Mechanical Engineering.

Students are able to make the programme more personal during the second semester, by choosing a minor. Some of the minors offered at Osprey are:

  • Flexible Energy Technology
  • International Biomedical Engineering
  • Engineering and Management

Forth year

In the first semester of the 4th year you choose one of our specializations:

  • Energy- and Process Technology
  • Business Engineering

In the 2nd semester of the 4th year you will do your graduation semester (20 weeks). Again you will work and study in a company in the field of Mechanical Engineering. During this final semester you also need to write your bachelor thesis report.
