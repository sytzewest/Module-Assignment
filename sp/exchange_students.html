Exchange

Are you ready to broaden your horizons and do you want extra challenges during your studies? Then choose an Exchange with one of our partner schools. You can exchange with a student to the school of you choice and experiance their culture and way of life.

The reasons to do an exchange.

  • 1. You experiance diffirent cultures.
  • 2. You see the world from a diffirent perspective.
  • 3. You grow to be a indipendant person.
  • 4. You become acquainted with diffirent ways of teaching and subjects.
  • 5. You improve your chances of inding a job.

Langauges

Many partner schools offer their education in English, this can also be another language, for example Spanish, French or German at many locations such as finland, uk, china and brazil. The exchange course is the perfect opportunity to get you ready for an international future.
