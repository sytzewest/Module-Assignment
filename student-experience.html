Discover Emmen


Living in the Netherlands

Osprey University is a truly international university. Want to get a glimpse of what student life is like within Osprey University? Then you have come the right place!


The university is home to students of many different cultures, as a whopping 25% of our 2000 students are from outside of the Netherlands. Emmen is a small town where one can truly enjoy the student life.


A true student town


Emmen has lots to offer its student population: shopping, cinemas, sports, and arts and culture. The city has grown into a high-tech hub and offers a stimulating environment for students and professionals alike.


Emmen has some very nice hot spots to enjoy a night out, such as Café de Zwetser and Lounge13. If you enjoy culture Emmen offers new events every week. And to stay fit you can exercise at various sports clubs and facilities with the Sports Pass.


HBO Sport

Unlimited Sports for a student price of only €10 a month!


With the HBO-Sports pass, you have unlimited access to a range of sports and activities in Emmen, Assen and Meppel for only €10 per month. This is the best-value sports pass in the Netherlands! You will be allowed to use any sports facilities in your own city, as well as in the other two cities. For instance, are you studying in Emmen, but do you live in Assen? You have access to unlimited sports in both cities!


In addition to unlimited access to sports, you are eligible to receive a discount on a range of activities. What to think of discounts on playing golf for a day, skiing, snowboarding, ice-skating, water-skiing, or kart racing? On top of that, you have access to discounted tennis, squash or diving courses.
