Why Osprey


Osprey University is a truly international university. Our staff is committed to provide you a meaningful, rigorous and quality graduate experiences in a unique environment.

The university is home to students of many different cultures, as a whopping 25% of our 2000 students are from outside of the Netherlands.


You can expand your intellectual horizons while deepening your expertise in an array of disciplines. You can learn new and interesting subjects in a challenging environment. Disciplinary and interdisciplinary challenges provide a unique learning and research environment for our graduate programme.

Osprey University aims to provide an international academic environment with an appreciable number of courses for students.


We provide sound personal education for hundreds of thousands of students. We’ll do our utmost to give you the best possible support. And if you can and want to do more, we’ll challenge you every step of the way.


Our high-quality programs provide an outstanding academic foundation to launch your career. You will work together with students from all around the world, join courses taught by excellent lecturers and researchers, and develop your ability to conduct scientific research.
