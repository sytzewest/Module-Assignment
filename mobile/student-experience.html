Living in the Netherlands

Osprey University is a truly international university. Want to get a glimpse of what student life is like within Osprey University? Then you have come the right place!


A beautiful night view of the city of Emmen


Emmen has lots to offer its student population: shopping, cinemas, sports, and arts and culture. Emmen is a small town where one can truly enjoy the student life. But don't take it from us, below you will find the accounts of our current students:


Federica Lowes - Aerospace (3rd year student)

"Osprey and the Aerospace course really grabbed my attention. I immediately got the feeling that this was the place for me. Both the university and the study programme were perfect for me. After the whole process of signing up for the study programme and moving to the Netherlands, I began my studies at Osprey. It really met my expectations, I even think it exceeded them."

Alex Lopez - Mechnical Engineering (2nd year student)

"I like the Design-Based Learning (DBL) system. Each module, you have a new class and a new tutor, because everyone is culturally different, you learn to accept them and the way they think. In the beginning, it can be hard to adjust to a new class, but that’s part of the learning process. It’s really cool that you get to know all the other students really well."

James Cook - Banking and Insurance (1st year student)

"The thought of studying abroad and living so far away from home was intimidating at first: for the first time I would be completely by myself and would have to start from the beginning when it came to making new friends and learning about a new city while balancing my university work. I can safely say, however, that my experiences during the first few months at Osprey University have been memorable and I know that making the decision to study in the Netherlands was the correct one."
