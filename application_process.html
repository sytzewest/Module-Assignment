How to apply


STEP 1: Choose a programme


Did your already choose your Bachelorâ€™s programme? If yes, please continue with step 2. Otherwise select a programme of your choice.

STEP 2: Check the admission requirements


Make sure you meet the admission requirements (academic & language requirements).

STEP 3: Check the application deadlines


Different application deadlines apply to different types of applicants.

STEP 4: collect the required application document


To start your online application procedure you will need several documents. Please note that you have to submit these documents digitally. If any hardcopy documents are required our Admission Office will notify you and tell you where to send them to. Here is a list of the documents that you need:


  • Passport
  • Secondary school diploma (in the original language and translated in English or Dutch)
  • Secondary school transcript of records (in the original language and translated in English or Dutch)
  • English language test (TOEFL/IELTS/CAMBRIDGE)
  • Motivation letter
  • CV

STEP 5: Apply via Studielink


Apply via Studielink by choosing a programme and a starting date. After your application via Studielink you will receive an important email with login credentials for the OSIRIS application where you can upload your documents. If you want to make a second application please use the Studielink account you already have.

STEP 6: Submit your application via Osiris


Continue your application via the OSIRIS application, using the login credentials provided to you via email. After you have logged in to Osiris please click on "details" behind your application to continue the procedure.
